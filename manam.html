One coast, one batch

“We met as strangers from two branches. We left as one group — with a hundred photos and a thousand inside jokes.”

Two years go quickly when you're not counting.

NIT Calicut handed us deadlines, vivas and a campus beside the sea — but mostly it handed us each other. Six people who might never have crossed paths, dropped into the same batch, ended up sharing everything from circuit diagrams to dinner plans, from exam stress to that one boat ride no one will let the others forget.

Now the batch is done and the map has opened up. Nagaraju and Varshitha are back in Telangana, Anvesh, Akshay and Hanock across Andhra Pradesh, and Shabarish all the way down in Tamil Nadu. The distances are real — but so is the habit of checking in.

So consider this our little headquarters: a place for the photos we keep meaning to share, the addresses we keep meaning to save, and the reunions we keep meaning to plan. Stay in touch. Send the card. Make the trip. కలిసి ఉంటే కలదు సుఖం — together, there is happiness.
